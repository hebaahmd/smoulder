  • Trim the wick to 5mm before every burn to ensure a clean, smoke-free flame.
  • On first use, burn until the wax pool reaches the edges of the jar — this prevents tunneling.
  • Never burn for more than 4 hours at a time. Let the candle cool fully before re-lighting.
  • Keep away from drafts, children, and pets. Always burn on a stable, heat-resistant surface.
  • Stop burning when 1cm of wax remains to protect the jar and surface below.
  • Once finished, clean the jar with warm water and repurpose it as a planter or storage vessel.
