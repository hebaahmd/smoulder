Scented Candles

Why Smoulder?

Crafted with intention. Burned with love.

Every candle we make is hand-poured, small-batch, and formulated to fill your space with warmth — not chemicals.

100% Natural Soy Wax

We use only premium, sustainably sourced soy wax — clean-burning, vegan, and biodegradable.

Hand-Poured Every Batch

No machines, no mass production. Each candle is poured by hand in small batches to guarantee quality.

Phthalate-Free Fragrance

Our fragrance oils are skin-safe, cosmetic-grade, and free of any harmful phthalates or parabens.

Up to 60-Hour Burn Time

A longer wick life means more moments of calm. Our candles are engineered to burn slowly and evenly.

Gift-Ready Packaging

Every order arrives in our signature kraft gift box — perfect for gifting right out of the bag.

Eco-Conscious & Reusable Jars

Our vessels are designed to be reused as planters, storage, or décor long after the candle is gone.
